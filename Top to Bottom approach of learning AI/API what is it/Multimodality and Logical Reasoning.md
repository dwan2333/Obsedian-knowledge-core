Language models are not just text predictors; they are multi-dimensional reasoning engines.

- **Solving Math:** AI doesn't calculate like a calculator. It recognizes the _patterns_ of logic. By using "Chain of Thought" (thinking step-by-step), it narrows down mathematical possibilities layer by layer to reach a conclusion.
    
- **Processing Images:** Through multimodality, vision systems chop images into patches and convert pixels into mathematical vectors.
    
- **The Universal Vector Space:** Words, code, math formulas, and images are all mapped as coordinates in the same massive geometric space. Because everything is reduced to universal mathematical tokens, the AI can seamlessly translate a visual screenshot into a Python script.
