# The Course: Serious Hold 'Em Strategy

_Ed Miller, 2015 — A structured curriculum for beating small-stakes no-limit hold 'em_

---

## Overview

**The Course** by Ed Miller is a systematic guide to winning at live small-stakes no-limit hold 'em. Rather than teaching poker as a collection of tips, Miller frames it as a **curriculum of skills** — each part of the book builds a layer on top of the last, from big-picture concepts down to specific in-game decisions.

The book is organized by stake level. Each section introduces the key skill needed to beat that level, then drills it with examples.

## Key Takeaways

- Poker at small stakes is beatable with **systematic, patient play** — not with reads or elaborate bluffs.
- Each stake level requires mastering a specific set of skills before moving up.
- The foundation is understanding **where money comes from** and how to extract it consistently.
